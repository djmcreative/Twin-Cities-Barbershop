01 About the Shop

Not your ordinary neighborhood barbershop.

At Twin Cities Barber Co., we provide high-quality services in a clean and professional setting. The chairs are old-school. The standards are higher. The conversations are unhurried.

Whether it's your first time in or you've been sitting in the same chair for years, you'll get the same thing: real craft, real care, and a sharp finish. No shortcuts, no fuss — just good barbering.

Skilled Barbers

Trained on the classics — fades, tapers, scissor work, hot-lather shaves — and dialed in on whatever you've been scrolling.

Welcoming Space

Clean, professional, and unpretentious. Bring a kid, bring a dad, bring a date. Everyone's welcome in our chair.

05 House Rules

Keep it smooth.

A few small things that keep the shop running for everyone in the chair — and everyone waiting for it.

01

Cancellations

If you have to cancel or change your appointment, just let us know. Less than 2 hours before your scheduled time, you'll be required to pre-pay for future appointments and we cannot guarantee a slot. Repeat late cancellations are non-refundable.

02

Late Arrivals

Please be courteous to staff and other clients — arrive on time. If you're more than 15 minutes late, we may have to reschedule. Running behind? Give us a call so we can minimize disruption to the rest of the day.

03

Walk-Ins

Walk-ins are welcome based on availability, but appointments are strongly recommended — especially Friday afternoons and Saturdays. The fastest way to lock in a chair is to book online.

04

Bringing Kids

Father-and-son cuts are a Twin Cities tradition. Kids are welcome any day we're open — just book both chairs together so we can keep things on schedule.
